Learn to run AI like a team — not a chatbot.

Kebanyakan orang nyangkut nyuruh AI satu-satu kayak asisten. Lompatannya: belajar mimpin — kasih aturan, sambungin ke sistem kamu, lalu pimpin sekawanan agent yang kerja sendiri. Aidia Academy ngajarin lompatan itu, hands-on, dari pengalaman ngerjain beneran.

Most people stay stuck telling AI what to do one task at a time, like an assistant. The leap is learning to direct it — set the rules, connect it to your systems, then lead a squad of agents that work on their own. Aidia Academy teaches that leap, hands-on, from real work.
